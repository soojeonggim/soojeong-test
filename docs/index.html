Queen's Journey

  • 1970

    Formation of Queen by Freddie Mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon.

  • 1973

    Release of their self-titled debut album, "Queen."

  • 1975

    Release of "A Night at the Opera," featuring the iconic song "Bohemian Rhapsody."

  • 1977

    "We Are the Champions" and "We Will Rock You" released, becoming anthems for sporting events worldwide.

  • 1980

    Release of "The Game," featuring hits "Another One Bites the Dust" and "Crazy Little Thing Called Love."

  • 1985

    Historic performance at Live Aid concert, which is often voted as the greatest live show in the history of rock.

  • 1991

    Freddie Mercury passes away, just one day after publicly acknowledging he had AIDS.
